Increased adoption and robust implementation of *highly performant and reliable* Bulk FHIR APIs are fundamental, not optional, for enabling a wide array of critical use cases. These include effective population health management, value-based care analytics, large-scale clinical research, timely public health reporting, and the development and training of responsible AI/ML models in healthcare.
